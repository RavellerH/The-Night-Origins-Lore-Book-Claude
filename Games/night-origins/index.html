I run south. The crystal from the river is still in my pocket. I take it out and hold it while I walk.

It is warm. That is wrong — everything I made tonight was cold. This one has been warm since the river, since the morning, since before anything happened. I hold it and I walk south into Vleyrra. The trees close behind me. I don't look back.

Avery Blue Alysdair · Vleyrra Forest · two days south of everything she knew
