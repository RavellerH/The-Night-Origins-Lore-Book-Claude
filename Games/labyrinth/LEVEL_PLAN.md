# Labyrinth — Level & Floor Plan

This document details the layout, atmosphere, and narrative progression for all 10 floors of the Labyrinth.

---

## Chapter 1: The Descent (Avery’s POV)

### Floor 1: The Demon Corridors
*   **Visuals:** Dark jagged stone, floating purple embers, distant lava glow.
*   **Narrative:** Avery and Nate wake up separated from the others. Introduction to the Deepborn "leak."
*   **Enemies:** Corrupted Imp, Dark Creeper.
*   **Loot:** Healing Drop (x2), Rune Fragment (Avery's Research).

### Floor 2: The Echo Chamber
*   **Visuals:** Walls with glowing purple veins that pulse in time with the music.
*   **Narrative:** Encounter **Frank**. To the player, it's been 20 mins; to Frank, 3 days. He is terrified of the "shadows."
*   **Enemies:** Dark Creeper, Shadow Echo (new).
*   **Loot:** Crystal Shard (consumable), Frank's Tattered Note.

### Floor 3: The Corrupted Grove
*   **Visuals:** Sickly green moss over stone, pulsating dark vines.
*   **Narrative:** Find **Alyse**. She is faint and tainted by the environment. Avery uses **Purify** for the first time.
*   **Enemies:** Mindless Drone (new), Corrupted Wolf.
*   **Loot:** Purifying Drop (x2), Focus: Light Pendant (for Nate).

### Floor 4: The Hall of Silence
*   **Visuals:** Smooth, black obsidian walls. No ambient noise.
*   **Narrative:** Find **Ellen**. She has been alone for a subjective week and is near a breaking point.
*   **Enemies:** Tainted Mage (Elite), Void Sentinel.
*   **Loot:** Crystal Lens (Focus for Avery), Ellen's Broken Ribbon.

## Chapter 2: The Other Path (Fran’s POV)

### Floor 5: The Temporal Rift
*   **Visuals:** Shifting blue/white architecture. Paths that appear and disappear.
*   **Narrative:** Switch to Fran, Myst, and Lynne. Lynne explains the elven secret of the broken seals.
*   **Enemies:** Phase Wraith, Temporal Shade.
*   **Loot:** Time Fragment (x3), Origin Bloom.

### Floor 6: The Memory Vault
*   **Visuals:** Floating stone tablets etched with runes.
*   **Narrative:** Fran uses **Bond Sense** to find a path through a "memory" of Earth.
*   **Enemies:** Echo Demon, Mind Rend Wraith.
*   **Loot:** Soul Thread (Charm for Myst), Memory Fragment.

### Floor 7: The Void Bridge
*   **Visuals:** A narrow bridge over an endless black abyss.
*   **Narrative:** Fran senses Avery’s presence above. The "Twin Aura" realization.
*   **Enemies:** Deepborn Guard, Void Sentinel.
*   **Loot:** Origin Gem (Focus for Fran), Ward Stone.

---

## Chapter 3: The Convergence (Combined Party)

### Floor 8: The Underworld Meeting
*   **Visuals:** Massive elven pillars supporting a "lava sky."
*   **Narrative:** **THE MEETING.** Avery and Fran unite. "Then you are my past!" / "What?!"
*   **Enemies:** Mixed demonic hordes.
*   **Special:** Unlock **Combo Skills** (Origin Crystal).

### Floor 9: The Heart’s Outskirts
*   **Visuals:** Red and black stone. The walls feel organic, like a beating heart.
*   **Narrative:** The two parties realize the city above is in danger as the seals fail further.
*   **Enemies:** Deepborn Lieutenant (Elite), Corruption Knight.
*   **Loot:** Deepborn Essence (Core), Greater Healing Drop.

### Floor 10: The Breach (Final Floor)
*   **Visuals:** A massive vortex of violet energy tearing through the floor.
*   **Narrative:** Battle with the **Deep Lord Fragment**.
*   **Enemies:** The Corruptor, Void Sentinels.
*   **Boss:** Deep Lord Fragment (3-Phase).

---

## Epilogue: The Siege of Light

### Mission: The Outskirts Flight
*   **Visuals:** Burning spires of the City of the Blinding Light under a storm sky.
*   **Narrative:** Escape the Labyrinth and find the city under siege by Zachariel.
*   **Objective:** Protect civilians and the injured Alyse.
*   **Enemies:** Zachariel’s Soldiers, Lightning Mages.
*   **Ending:** Cinematic retreat into the Huge Woods.
