# Concept Art Prompts — Kalmirth

> Image generation prompts for expanding the Lore Navigator gallery and series concept art.

## Style Anchor

Add this to the end of every prompt:

> *dark fantasy concept art, painterly digital illustration, impressionistic brushwork, volumetric atmospheric light, massive environmental scale with small human figures, deep shadow and muted rich palette, dramatic perspective — style of: Jakub Różalski, Jessica Rossier, Marc Simonetti*

### Characters

**Faeron — The Last Elven General**
> An ancient elven warrior standing alone at the edge of a ruined battlefield, back to the viewer. Centuries-old armor, weathered and vine-covered. He faces the remnants of a destroyed forest — blackened stumps stretching to the horizon under a grey sky. The only figure in a vast broken landscape. Mood: survivor's grief, unending vigil. Palette: ash grey, dead brown, faint silver light from overcast sky. *[+ style anchor]*

**Castor — Fire Mage Warlord**

Behind him: a fire mage encampment at night, tents glowing orange, soldiers moving. His face lit entirely by flame — half in fierce gold light, half in deep shadow. Mood: commanding, dangerous, absolute authority. Palette: deep orange, char black, ember red. *[+ style anchor]*

**Alyse — Zachariel's Lost Sister**
> A young woman, teenage, caught mid-motion in a forest corridor at dusk. She's looking back over her shoulder, expression unreadable — curiosity or fear or both. The forest is closing around her, the path behind already swallowed by dark. The last light of day is ahead of her, not behind. A single pale crystal glints at her throat. Mood: the moment before something changes forever. Palette: deep forest green, dusk amber, cool shadow. *[+ style anchor]*

**Mihr — The Goddess of Nevella**
> A divine figure standing at the edge of a sky island, robes moving in wind that doesn't exist at ground level. She faces outward over an endless cloud ocean, one hand raised, not in gesture but in permission — something invisible departing. Her face is calm in the way that is older than emotion. Below the clouds: the faint curve of a continent. Pale blue and silver light emanates from her, not glowing but *present*. Palette: pale blue-white, silver, cloud grey, distant gold. *[+ style anchor]*

### Story Moments

**The Alliance Oath Circle**
> Six figures standing in a loose circle in a forest clearing at night, each radiating their faction's magic — soft gold light, green pulse, flickering flame, shadow curl, dark shimmer. The light sources overlap at the center, mixing into something new. The forest looms enormous around them; the figures are small against the ancient trees. Aerial-ish perspective, looking down slightly. Mood: fragile unity, historic weight. Palette: deep forest green, night black, six distinct magic color pools meeting at center. *[+ style anchor]*

**Avery — Crystal Encasement**
> A young woman suspended inside a massive translucent crystal formation, arms loose at her sides, eyes closed. The crystal catches light from somewhere unseen, refracting it into cold blue and violet shards across the cave floor. Figures stand watching from outside — tiny, reverent, afraid. The crystal is not a prison; it looks like it is *protecting* something. Mood: sacrifice and dormancy. Palette: cold crystal blue, deep cave shadow, distant warm torchlight. *[+ style anchor]*

**The HQ Infiltration — Daun Hitam Glow**
> A single figure moving through a long corridor inside a stone facility — Black Leaf architecture, angular and institutional. The figure's hands are trailing dark leaf-shaped shadows that cling to the walls and floor, spreading as she moves. Doors ahead are sealed; she doesn't slow. The corridor lights flicker and die behind her as the darkness follows. Mood: unstoppable, quiet destruction. Palette: institutional grey stone, ink black spreading, single cold blue emergency light still burning ahead. *[+ style anchor]*

**Avery and Fran — The Underworld Meeting**
> Two young women standing on a cracked stone platform in an enormous underground void — the Underworld. They've just met. Both look up at the same time, faces lit from below by faint blue-white light rising from the abyss beneath them. Above: nothing. Around them: carved stone pillars descending into the dark. They are very small in a very large place. Mood: recognition, strangeness, the weight of coincidence. Palette: deep void black, cold upward light, stone grey, two figures' warm skin tones as the only warmth. *[+ style anchor]*

### Locations

**Gyorellya Forest — Ancient Shrine**
> A ruined stone shrine at the heart of a dense, ancient forest — trees so large their canopies form a ceiling. The shrine is half-swallowed by roots and vines but still upright. Carved into its face: elven script and a relief of figures in a circle. Pale light filters down through a single gap in the canopy, illuminating the shrine's center. No people. The forest is watching. Palette: deep green, filtered silver-white light, moss grey stone, shadow. *[+ style anchor]*

---

**The Underworld — Dunia Bawah**
> A vast underground space seen from above — an immense cave system, the ceiling lost in darkness, the floor far below covered in faint bioluminescent formations. Stone bridges and natural arches connect platforms at different heights. A river of slow black water moves through the lowest level, reflecting something that should have no light source. Scale established by two tiny figures on a mid-level bridge. Mood: ancient, alien, not hostile but fundamentally not for living things. Palette: void black, cold blue-white bioluminescent glow, deep violet shadows, stone grey. *[+ style anchor]*

---

**Elven Ruins — Alliance Meeting Ground**
> The interior of a partially collapsed elven structure — high vaulted ceiling open to the sky through a long break, moonlight falling in a single column onto a stone floor covered in old inscriptions. Alliance faction members sit or stand around the light column in rough council. The ruins stretch in every direction, enormously old. The roof is largely gone; stars above. Mood: making new decisions in old places, the past as context for the present. Palette: silver moonlight, night blue sky above, warm torch orange from the figures, deep stone shadow. *[+ style anchor]*

### Concepts / Objects

**Zorgerath — The Amulet**
> Extreme closeup concept study: a dark metal amulet, tarnished and angular, with a cracked purple crystal set at its center. The crystal pulses — faint inner light, deep violet, rhythmic like breathing. The metal is carved with script too old to read. Around it: residual shadow that doesn't match the light direction. Resting on stone. Mood: dormant power, ancient corruption, something aware inside. Palette: tarnished dark metal, deep purple crystal glow, black shadow. *[+ style anchor]*

---

**The Totem Stones — Network Node**
> An ancient stone monolith standing at a crossroads in open countryside, carved with layered elven-style geometric patterns. It is old — older than the forest that has grown around it, older than the road that now runs past it. Faint lines of light trace the carved patterns, so dim most travellers would miss them entirely. Night scene. The road is empty. Stars above. Mood: ancient infrastructure hiding in plain sight, the world's skeleton visible to those who know to look. Palette: grey stone, night blue, faint silver-gold trace light in the carvings. *[+ style anchor]*
