### Writing style reminders
- No filter words: felt, saw, heard, noticed, thought, realized, seemed
- Show behavior not emotion labels
- Speech tags: only `said` and `asked`
- Short sentences. One thing at a time.
- Action beats: separate sentences ending in periods
- Metaphors from this world, not generic banks
- Avery voice: dry, precise, involuntary humor, accurate observation, no self-pity
